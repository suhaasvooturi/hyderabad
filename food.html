Haleem

A special dish during Ramzan. It’s a slow-cooked stew made from wheat, lentils, and meat. Rich, spicy, and super satisfying!

Famous Foods of Hyderabad

Hyderabadi Biryani

The crown jewel of Hyderabad! This rice dish is made with basmati rice, meat (usually chicken or mutton), saffron, and spices. It’s layered, slow-cooked, and full of flavor.

Double Ka Meetha

This is Hyderabad’s take on bread pudding. Made with fried bread, milk, sugar, dry fruits, and saffron — it’s sweet, soft, and royal!
